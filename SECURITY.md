# Security Policy

## Our Commitment to Security
The 3.5% Movement and the National Renewal Fund (NRF) take security and privacy seriously. We are committed to protecting our systems, our contributors, and the integrity of the project. This repository is currently in active development, and we welcome responsible disclosure from the security community.

## Reporting a Vulnerability
If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it privately and responsibly. Do not open a public Issue or discuss the vulnerability in public forums.

Please report vulnerabilities using the following process:

1. Email a detailed report to: the3point5@tutamail.com  
2. Include a clear description of the issue, its potential impact, and steps to reproduce it  

We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours and aim to provide a remediation timeline within 7 days.

## Safe Harbor
We will not pursue legal action against individuals who:

- Report vulnerabilities to us privately and in good faith  
- Provide reasonable time for remediation before public disclosure  
- Avoid privacy violations, data extraction, or service disruption  
- Do not exploit vulnerabilities for personal or thirdâ€‘party gain  

## Scope
The following are considered in-scope for responsible disclosure:

- Public-facing components of this repository  
- Public documentation  
- Public endpoints or interfaces (when applicable)

## Out of Scope
The following activities are strictly prohibited:

- Any testing that risks compromising supporter data or private systems  
- Social engineering or phishing of volunteers, contributors, or staff  
- Denial-of-service (DoS) or stress testing  
- Physical attacks against hosting or infrastructure providers  

## Response Times
We aim to:

- Acknowledge all reports within **48 hours**  
- Provide a remediation plan or timeline within **7 days**
